sketch 7

This sketch represents driving to school. For me it is 32 miles to drive to school. When I see there is traffic I tend to leave earlier so the red bar has a head start because that one left earlier and the green bar represent a car that goes through no traffic. I put the random as less than or equals to because with traffic it is about double the time than without traffic. When they make it to the 32 mile mark it says arrived. I thought it was cool to experiment with the timing of when the two bars can fill at the same time changing the start position and the probability of it.
