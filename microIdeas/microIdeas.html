Micro-Project Ideas

Idea 1 - Ride to School

I will make a person which represents a square and it will move from either left to right or the other way around and will choose a ride to make it to school. And choosing will determine whether you make it to school on time or not. Its going to start with space button and as it is moving you press the space again to choose the ride each ride will take a certain amount of time. There will be things like bikes, buses, carpool and they will each stay consistent with how much time they will take. If they make it before the timer ends then it will say "You made it to school on time!" and if you arrive to school then it will say "You got to school late!".There will be multiple stages and if you get the correct rides then you will make it on time. There will be uneveness after the first stage because it will be depending on what you choose on that first stage. The feeling is supposed to be a waiting but rushed feeling.

Idea 2 - Drive to School

This will be similar to ride to school but instead of choosing the ride you choose the rode but it will be more random because you cant tell where the road will take you so you wont know if you will even get to school. Because you wont know the difference between the different roads you will get a random amount of time taken off so you wont have really control if you get to school on time or not. If they make it before the timer ends then it will say "You made it to school on time!" and if you arrive to school then it will say "You got to school late!". Will start with space and you can press space again to stop where the car will go. Just like the ride to school the uneveness will start after the first stage. The feeling is more uncertain and rushed again.
