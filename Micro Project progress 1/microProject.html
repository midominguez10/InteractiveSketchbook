Ride to School

Part 1

Interaction starts with the space button. The only thing I have for now is the circle that will be moving back and forth. X starts at 500 in a 1000,1000 frame so it starts in the middle. The timer decreases at every frame. The x position of the circle updates. Whenever it reaches the end of the frame it goes the opposite direction.

If you press space again under those shapes they will take time off your timer. The tension in this system is the timer.
