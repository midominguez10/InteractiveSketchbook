The up and down arrow keys change the image. Each image shows a text telling us the image number and it also lets us know when the cycle restarts. What I learned was that the keyCode command can be any key on your keyboard and it can open up many possibilities if you are creative enough.

After doing sketch 9 this new method of showing the text on the sketch just makes the code simpler and more consistent than adding a if currentIndex equals the number. The new behavior that was added was moving the images to the center and including instructions at the bottom which also communicates better than what sketch 8 had.
